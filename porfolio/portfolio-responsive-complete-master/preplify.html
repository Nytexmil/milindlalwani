Preplify

A condensed resource for high schoolers

Preplify is an online web application that started off as an internship catalog for high schoolers. Many of my friends had issues finding good internships as high schoolers and didn't know where to go. And those that got internships had connections. After scraping the internet for internships, our program cataloged 50+ internships and summer programs into a database that has full search and filter compatibility.

I later expanded this by adding a a catalog for volunteering events from different public schools across NYC, this allowed for increased volunteer turnout for many programs and streamlined access to these events. The program automatically removes old programs and scrapes the web for popular and newer events.
